Coaching tips
Give me 3 specific strategies to improve wait time and gender equity in questioning.
AI

These are genuinely high-leverage moves · and none of them require extra planning. Here are three things worth experimenting with in your next class:

1. Silent countdown · After posing a question, hold up your hand and silently count to 5 before accepting responses. It feels a little uncomfortable at first (that's normal!), but students quickly learn the expectation and their answers get noticeably deeper. Teachers in a 2023 TNTP study went from 1.8s → 4.2s within two weeks just by doing this.

2. Randomizer for cold calls · Wheel of Names or ClassDojo's selector takes 30 seconds to set up. It removes the unconscious pull toward the same students and signals to the whole room: everyone is expected to think. Your class will feel different on day one.

3. Whiteboards before voices · Have every student write their answer first, then share. This guarantees everyone has formed a thought before anyone speaks · and it gives you a quick visual scan to see who's ready and who might need support.
