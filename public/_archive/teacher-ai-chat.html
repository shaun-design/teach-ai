Give me 3 specific strategies to improve wait time and gender equity in questioning.
AI

Here are three targeted strategies based on your lesson:

1. Countdown think time · After posing a question, hold up fingers and silently count to 5 before taking responses. This simple visual cue helped teachers in a 2023 TNTP study increase wait time from 1.8s → 4.2s within 2 weeks.

2. Popsicle sticks or class randomizer · Use a random name selector (Wheel of Names, ClassDojo, or physical sticks) for cold calls. This removes unconscious bias and signals to all students that they're expected to participate.

3. Whiteboard responses · Have all students write answers on mini whiteboards before any verbal sharing. This ensures every student formulates a response independently, and you can scan the room for equity before selecting who speaks aloud.
