The problem we solve

Professional learning is broken.
TeachAI fixes it.

Most professional development is generic, infrequent, and disconnected from what's actually happening in classrooms.

Coaches can't be everywhere

A single instructional coach supports 20 to 30 teachers. Formal observations happen 2 to 3 times a year, leaving months of teaching invisible to coaching support.

Feedback arrives too late

By the time a coach observes and writes feedback, teachers have already taught the same lesson patterns dozens of times. Real-time insight is the only way to change habits.

Generic PD doesn't stick

One-size-fits-all professional development misses what each teacher actually needs. Teachers deserve feedback tied to their own practice, not someone else's.

TeachAI gives every teacher a coaching feedback loop every single week
How it works

Built for teachers and coaches

Two purpose-built experiences that work together: teachers get real-time AI guidance, and coaches get the signal to focus where it matters most.

1
Submit your lesson

Upload a video, lesson plan, exit tickets, or student work samples. TeachAI accepts the artifacts that actually capture your teaching practice.

2
AI analyzes your classroom

TeachAI measures student talk time, wait time, how often you ask deeper questions (analysis and reasoning, not just recall), and a dozen other research-backed indicators, automatically and without manual input.

3
Get personalized feedback

Receive specific, actionable feedback tied to your lesson, not generic tips. Your coach reviews and approves it before it reaches you.

4
Chat with your AI coaching assistant

Ask follow-up questions, explore strategies, and plan for your next lesson in a conversation that knows your complete teaching history.

Platform capabilities

Everything you need to grow great teachers

TeachAI combines classroom analytics, AI-generated feedback, and coaching workflow tools in one integrated platform.

Video lesson analysis

Upload classroom videos and let TeachAI automatically measure student talk time, questioning techniques, wait time, and more, frame by frame.

Artifact review

Lesson plans, exit tickets, and student work are analyzed alongside video to give a complete picture of instructional quality.

AI coaching conversations

An always-available AI assistant that knows every lesson, every piece of feedback, and every goal, so teachers get personalized guidance anytime.

AI-drafted feedback

Coaches approve high-quality, data-backed feedback drafts instead of writing from scratch, cutting feedback time from hours to minutes.

Growth tracking

Every teacher has a longitudinal record of indicators, feedback, and milestones. Spot growth, celebrate wins, and identify when someone needs extra support.

School-wide pattern recognition

TeachAI identifies clusters of shared challenges and emerging strengths, helping coaches decide when to schedule group sessions and what to present at PD.
